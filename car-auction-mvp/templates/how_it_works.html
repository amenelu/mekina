How Mekina Works

Our platform is designed to be the simplest and most transparent way to buy or sell cars in Ethiopia. Whether you're a buyer looking for your dream car or a seller ready to find a new home for your vehicle, hereâ€™s how it works.

For Buyers

1

Find Your Car in Two Ways

Browse Listings: Explore live auctions, fixed-price sales, and rental cars on our comprehensive All Listings page.
Submit a Request: Can't find it? Use our Car Request feature. Tell us exactly what you want, or let our guided process help you decide. Our network of verified dealers will then send you tailored offers.

2

Engage and Bid

Ask sellers questions directly on any listing to get all the details you need. For auctions, place your bid and stay on top. For dealer offers on your car request, you can ask questions about each specific offer to make an informed decision.

3

Win & Finalize the Deal

If you're the highest bidder when an auction ends, or if you accept a dealer's offer, congratulations! We connect you directly with the seller to arrange payment and vehicle handover. All listings are reviewed by our team to ensure quality and transparency.

For Sellers & Dealers

1

Submit Your Car

Use our simple submission form to provide details and photos of your car. You can choose to list it for auction or for a fixed-price sale.

2

Admin Review

Our team reviews every submission to ensure quality and accuracy. You can edit your submission from your dashboard until it is approved.

3

Engage with Buyers

Once your listing is live, answer questions from potential buyers directly on your listing page. Dealers can also browse customer requests and place offers to win new business.

For Rental Companies

1

List Your Fleet

From your dedicated Rental Dashboard, use the submission form to list vehicles available for rent. Our team will review and approve your listings.

2

Manage Your Listings

Your dashboard provides a clear overview of all your rental cars and their approval status. Once live, customers can see your vehicles on the "All Listings" and "Rentals" pages and contact you directly.
