How Mekina Works

Our platform is designed to be the simplest and most transparent way to buy or sell enthusiast cars in Ethiopia. Whether you're a buyer looking for your dream car or a seller ready to find a new home for your vehicle, hereâ€™s how it works.

For Buyers

1

Browse or Request

Explore live auctions, fixed-price sales, and rental listings on our All Listings page. Can't find what you're looking for? Submit a Car Request and let our network of verified dealers find it for you.

2

Ask Questions & Place Bids

On any listing, you can ask the seller questions directly to get all the details you need. For auctions, place your bid and stay on top. For dealer offers on your request, you can ask questions about each specific offer to make an informed decision.

3

Win & Finalize the Deal

If you're the highest bidder when an auction ends, or if you accept a dealer's offer, congratulations! We connect you directly with the seller to arrange payment and vehicle handover. All listings are reviewed by our team to ensure quality and transparency.

For Sellers

1

Submit Your Car

Use our simple submission form to provide details and photos of your car. You can choose to list it for auction, for a fixed-price sale, or for rent.

2

Engage with Buyers

Once your listing is approved and live, you can answer questions from potential buyers directly on your listing page, building trust and providing clarity.
